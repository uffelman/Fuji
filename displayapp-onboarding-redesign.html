Page 2 — Permissions (not granted)
Welcome to DisplayApp
Accessibility Permission
Global keyboard shortcuts need Accessibility access so DisplayApp can respond to hotkeys even when another app is in focus.
⚠️
Permission not granted
Keyboard shortcuts will not work until this is enabled.
Or grant access manually:
1
Click Open System Settings below.
2
Scroll to find DisplayApp in the list.
3
Toggle the switch next to DisplayApp to on.
4
Return here — permission status updates automatically.
